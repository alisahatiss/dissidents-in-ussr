Letter of Twenty-Five Soviet Figures of Science and Culture to CPSU General Secretary L. I. Brezhnev Protesting the Trend toward Justifying the Policies of Stalin

This significant document represents one of the earliest collective protests against the rehabilitation of Stalin's policies. Signed by prominent Soviet intellectuals, scientists, and cultural figures, it marked a crucial moment in the development of organized dissent within the USSR.
