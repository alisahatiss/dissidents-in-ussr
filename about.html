About the Dissident Archive

"Dissident Archive" is a compact online encyclopedia devoted to the people who first challenged the Soviet system through protest letters in 1965–1968. We collect and publish concise, carefully verified biographies of more than five hundred signatories, reconstructing their social profiles, personal motives, and later destinies.

The project draws on a corpus of collective letters preserved through samizdat and the archives of Memorial International, as well as on memoirs and court documents.

Our Mission

Our aim is to enrich the history of Soviet dissent with new names and to broaden the global vocabulary of non-violent resistance. On the site you will find an intuitive search for people and letters, year-by-year visualizations of protest activity, and thematic collections for researchers, journalists, and anyone interested in freedom of expression in the USSR.

Future Development

In the future, the site will also host the full original texts of the petitions for open scholarly use.
